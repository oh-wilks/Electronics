Wiring 

Power
DFPlayer	Arduino Nano
VCC	        5V
GND	        GND

Audio

Option A – Speaker

DFPlayer	Speaker
SPK_1	    Speaker +
SPK_2	    Speaker –

Control pins (minimum)

DFPlayer	Arduino Nano
RX	        D10 (through 1kΩ resistor if you have one)
TX	        D11

(The resistor protects DFPlayer RX — not required for quick testing, but nice.)
